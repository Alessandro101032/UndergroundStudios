Despre noi - PerformX

Cine suntem?

PerformX started with a simple idea: to make sports science, training knowledge, and nutrition guidance easier to access for anyone who wants to improve their performance. As interest in fitness, health, and athletic optimization continues to grow, many people struggle to find clear, reliable, and practical information they can actually use in their daily training. PerformX was created to bridge that gap by combining sports technology with straightforward nutrition and performance insights.

What is our mission?

PerformX is a sport technology and nutrition platform built for people who want to train smarter, perform better, and live healthier. Our mission is to combine modern sports science, intelligent training tools, and practical nutrition knowledge to help athletes and active individuals reach their full potential.
